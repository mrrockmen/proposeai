FAQ

Questions, answered

Is there a free trial? +
Yes — the Starter plan is free forever with 5 proposals/month. Pro and Agency plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with no fees or penalties. Your proposals remain accessible until the end of your billing period.
How does the AI personalization work? +
ProposeAI analyzes your project brief, the client's industry, and your previous winning proposals to generate content that's tailored — not generic. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.
Can I use my own brand colors and logo? +
Yes, on Pro and Agency plans. Upload your logo, set your brand colors, and choose fonts — every proposal looks unmistakably yours.
What integrations are available? +
Agency plans include HubSpot, Pipedrive, Notion, Slack, and Zapier. Pro users get Zapier access. Full REST API available on Agency plans.
